What is battleball?

Battleball is the new and improved version of football. Do you think you understand the rules of football? Well, check out Battleball for a futuristic-type version of Football. Choose wisely when you make your moves, otherwise your opponents may out-smart you and maneuver around your carefully laid plans. Battleball brings your favorite sport into your home and makes it even more interesting to play over and over. Check out Battleball for a tough new version of football that will have you and your opponent battling to be the best.

Objective?

You are the coach of one of the world's finest future football teams. Lead your team to victory by being the first to score two touchdowns.
